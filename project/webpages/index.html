Sneaker culture is centered around a passion for footwear and the stories behind them, with enthusiasts often taking part in events and forums to discuss the latest trends. The origins of sneaker culture are rooted in the early days of athletic footwear, the rise of basketball, and the groundbreaking release of the Nike Air Jordan 1. These key moments set the stage for the commercialization and mainstreaming of sneaker culture, which would continue to evolve in the decades that followed.
